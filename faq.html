Frequently Asked Questions

What hosting plans does Cvahost offer?

Cvahost offers a variety of hosting plans, including shared hosting, cloud hosting, VPS servers, and reseller hosting. Each plan is designed to cater to different business needs and budgets.

Do you provide a money-back guarantee?

Yes, Cvahost provides a 30-day money-back guarantee on most of our hosting plans. This ensures that our customers can try our services risk-free.

How can I transfer my website to Cvahost?

We offer free migration services to help you transfer your website to Cvahost. Our support team will guide you through the process to ensure a seamless transition.

What support options are available?

Our support team is available 24/7 through live chat, email, and our ticketing system. We also provide a comprehensive knowledge base for self-help resources.

Does Cvahost provide domain registration services?

Yes, Cvahost offers domain registration services. You can register new domains or transfer existing ones to our platform with ease.

What security features are included in the hosting plans?

All Cvahost plans come with free SSL certificates, daily backups, malware scanning, and DDoS protection to ensure your website's security.

Can I upgrade my hosting plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your hosting plan at any time as your business grows. Our plans are flexible to accommodate your evolving needs.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept payments through credit cards, debit cards, net banking, and popular wallets to ensure convenience for our customers.

Do you provide backup services?

Yes, we offer daily automatic backups to ensure your data is always safe and recoverable in case of an emergency.

Can I host multiple websites?

Yes, depending on the hosting plan you choose, you can host multiple websites under one account with Cvahost.

What is the uptime guarantee?

Cvahost guarantees 99.9% uptime for all hosting plans, ensuring your website remains online and accessible at all times.
